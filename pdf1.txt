公司介绍 About Us
尤瑞克进出口有限公司（以下简称“ 尤瑞克” ）是一家根据加拿大阿尔伯塔省法律注册成立的公司，于2 0 1 6 年3 月1 日注册
成立，商业注册号为2 0 1 9 5 4 0 1 4 1 ，注册地址为加拿大阿尔伯塔省卡尔加里西北边山考特1 6 3 号。
尤瑞克通过多年的精心经营，积累了众多合作伙伴和丰富的贸易经验，在加拿大和海外拥有众多战略合作伙伴。其核心业务
分为四个板块：南非矿山开发、南非矿石贸易、美式家具研发& 设计、北美木材贸易，另外拥有A m o r  V i n c i t 和P h i l l y  两
个国际品牌商标。
经过不懈地开拓进取，公司已逐步发展成为集矿产投资、商品贸易、家具研发设计为一体的多元化现代企业。
Ulric Import Export Limitd (hereinafter referred to as "Ulric") is a company incorporated 
under the laws of the Province of Alberta, Canada, registered on March 1, 2016, with 
the business registration number of 2019540141 and the registered address of 163 
Edgehill Court NW Calgary, Alberta, Canada.
Ulric has accumulated many partners and rich trading experience through years of 
careful operation, and has many strategic partners in Canada and overseas. Ulric has 
divided its core business into four Sectors: South African Mine Development, South 
African Ore Trade, American Furniture R&D and Design, North American Lumber Trade. 
In addition, it owns two international brand trademarks, Amor Vincit and Philly.
Through unremitting pioneering and enterprising, the company has gradually 
developed into a diversified modern enterprise integrating mineral investment, 
commodity trade, furniture R&D and design.

PART 2 第二部分 
South African Mine Development 南非矿山开发  
南非拥有世界上最发达的采矿业之一，数十年来一直
是世界上许多矿产资源的主要生产国。公司提供全面
的采矿开发服务，并通过实施环境管理计划将作业对
环境的影响降至最低。目前，公司已成功投资、勘探
和开采了大量自然储量丰富的矿山。
South Africa has one of the world's most developed mining 
industries and has been the world's leading producer of 
many mineral resources for decades.The company provides 
comprehensive mining development services and minimizes 
the impact of operations on the environment through the 
implementation of the environmental management plan. At 
present, the company has successfully invested in, explored 
and mined numerous mines that contain considerable 
natural reserves.
South African Ore Trade 南非矿石贸易  
通过多年从事大宗商品业务，公司积累了丰富的贸易经验，
在国内外拥有众多合作伙伴。凭借南非独特的矿产资源优势，
通过经验丰富的贸易团队、先进的技术、可靠的服务和成熟
的销售渠道，我们服务于全球钢铁冶金和钒储能行业，为客
户提供优质经济的钢铁冶金和储能原材料。主要产品有：南
非钒钛磁铁矿、赤铁矿、铬矿、铜矿、锰矿等。
Through years of engaging in bulk commodity business, the company 
has accumulated rich trading experience and has many partners 
domestically and abroad. Relying on the unique mineral resource 
advantages in South Africa, and through the experienced trade team, 
advanced technology, reliable service and mature sales channels, we 
serve the global iron and steel metallurgy and vanadium energy 
storage industries, and provide customers with high-quality and 
economical raw materials for use in steel metallurgy and energy 
storage. The main products are: South African vanadium titanium 
magnetite, hematite, chrome ore, copper ore, manganese ore, etc.
American Furniture R&D and Design 美式家具研发&设计 
致力于传播美国生活方式的理念，我们坚持以超高性价比的价
格，为消费者提供纯正的美国家居风格，满足风格和质量的双
重追求。从细致的研发到精心的设计，我们致力于打造精致温
馨的家居空间，既体现风格，又体现格调。我们产品的核心价
值是实用主义和舒适，引领健康、舒适、自然、简单、优雅的
慢节奏家庭生活。我们的家具采用复古技术，融入历史和文化，
散发出经典的时尚设计。
Committed to spreading the concept of American lifestyle, we persistently 
provide consumers with an authentic American home style, at a super 
cost-effective price, satisfying the dual pursuits of style and quality. From 
meticulous research and development to careful design, we devote 
ourselves to creating exquisite and warm home spaces that expresses 
both style and exclusivity. The core values of our products are 
pragmatism and comfort, leading a healthy, comfortable, natural, simple 
and elegant slow-paced home life. Our furniture uses retro techniques, 
embraces history and culture, and exudes a classic fashion design.
North American Lumber Trade 北美木材贸易  
北美是世界上硬木材资源最丰富的地区，林业发展历史悠久，
可持续发展性强。该地区木材具有纹理天然、色泽自然的特点。
凭借得天独厚的地理和资源优势，先进的技术，统一的标准，
严格的分类，我们为市场提供优质的产品。主要产品有黄杨、
红橡、白橡、黑胡桃、加拿大杨树、松树等，已出口到中国、
越南、马来西亚、欧洲等地区。
North America is the region with the most abundant hardwood 
resources in the world as well as a long history of forestry development 
and strong product sustainability. Lumber in this region has the 
characteristics of natural texture, color and lustre. With unique 
geographical and resource advantages, advanced technology, unified 
standard, strict classification, we provide the market with excellent 
products. Our main products such as yellow poplar, red oak, white oak, 
black walnut, Canadian poplar, pine, etc., have been exported to China, 
Vietnam, Malaysia, Europe and other regions.
